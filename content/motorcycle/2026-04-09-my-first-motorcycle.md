## Background

I would like to provide some background about how and when I got into motorcycles.
I joined Bosch as a Junior Software Developer in 2019 straight after finishing my Bachelor's.
After 3 years, few fresher's joined my team and they had owned Royal Enfield's and they used to discuss regarding their passion about riding and i listened to their stories about their journey w.r.t buying their 1st motorcycle and enjoying it. 
This kind of sparked my inner feeling of owning the motorcycle and living with it. Truly speaking, i got **FOMO**. 
This discussions happended during end of 2022, finally i decided to buy one to enjoy and come out of work pressure and to feel good and find happiness in motorcycling.

I started test rides in start of 2023, my first test ride was recently popular RE at that time `Hunter 350`. I fell in love with that exhaust, i still love that exhaust till today. After that I test rode `TVS Ronin`, `Suzuki V-storm 250`, `KTM Duke 200`, `RE Classic 350`. Reason for choosing these specific models was i decided to buy something around budget of 2-2.5 Lakhs On-road price.
I didn't know about adventure riding back then, i wasn't into any kind of thing like touring, sport or adventure or cafe racing.
I just wanted to buy a bike which would make me feel happy. I shortlisted 3 bikes: Hunter 350, TVS Ronin and Suzuki V-storm 250.
v-storm was out of budget, on-road price was around 2.8L, Ronin looks were not attractive or stylist enough for me, so i decided to buy `Hunter 350`. Everyone in my gang had RE's, so this also kind of influenced my decision to go with this motorcycle.

I booked it on July 10th 2023 and received the delivery on July 30th 2023. City rides are super fun on this machine and occasional highway rides are cool breeze in the air, but long distance touring is kind of uneasy but feels good most of the time.

## Long Rides

### Ooty

My First Long ride was in `Marh 2024`. We travelled to Ooty from Bangalore, it was a `3-day trip`.
This ride will always be special to me because i learnt so many things and enjoyed it to the core.
I have 2 core memories which i would like to share:
- We reached stay in Ooty around 3:30PM in the afternoon, i was so stressed and my headache levels were too high, so i slept the whole day. I kind of expected this because i was constantly feeling stress along with joy the whole ride. As this is my first ever long ride i had so much anxiety and fear too. Fortunately this wasn't the case for future rides !!
- We were exploring offroad trails for fun during the day, one guy randomly started climbing one trail road, it was too steep and he fell down, so 2 others followed the same path to help him, eventually everyone went up the steep trail, i was the last one because everyone thought its not possible on Hunter, but they helped along the way. Funny thing is, there was a graveyard at the top and we took a picture of our motorcycles surrounded by that graveyard.
